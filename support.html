Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recover my StreetWise account?

To recover your account, visit our account recovery page and follow the steps provided. You will need to show that the account really belongs to the requester via email associated with your account, with the keyword.

What should I do if I encounter a bug in a game?

If you encounter a bug, please report it through our bug reporting system. Provide as much detail as possible, including steps to reproduce the issue, your system specifications, and any error messages you received.
